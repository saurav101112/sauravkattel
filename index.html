The Benefits of Organic Tea Farming in Nepal

Tea is one of the most popular beverages in the world, and Nepal is one of the major tea producers and exporters. However, most of the tea produced in Nepal is conventional, which relies on chemical fertilizers and pesticides that harm the environment and human health. In this article, I discuss the benefits of organic tea farming, which is a more sustainable and eco-friendly way of growing tea. I also share some examples and best practices of organic tea farming in Nepal and other countries.
