About & Background

Saurav Kattel is a dedicated horticultural scientist and agro-tech innovator based in Nepal, passionate about bridging the gap between modern agricultural science and the farmers who need it most.

Through Saurav Agro-Tech, he shares research-backed knowledge on organic farming, crop management, and sustainable practices tailored for Nepal's diverse climate zones — from the Terai plains to the high hills.

His work spans field research, educational content, and direct farmer support, transforming traditional agriculture into a modern, productive, and sustainable practice for Nepal's growing farming community.
